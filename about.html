FAQs
My Money is Deducted but order is not showing in Order Screen.
At times , servers of your Bank Account send late confirmation due to Security Checks or tech issues at their end. Unfortunately , we have to wait for their confirmation before showing your order as a success.
If My order is in rejected status , what will happen to my money?
If order is in rejected status then System has Already Generated the Refund Request and your money will be transferred back to your bank account / payment source.
What is the Refund Cycle of Payments?
Refund timelines depend on your Payment Source. Usually its 5-7 working days (excluding Bank Holidays or Public Holidays). Very rarely, few of the banks take more than that.

About Us

"In a world where "authentic biryani" is used more as a phrase rather than served as a dish, we at Biriyani Feast have made it our goal to serve truly authentic, Hyderabadi Biryani to those who know the taste that goes with that promise. Using the finest ingredients, our chefs cook a vast array of Tikkas, Kebabs and Curries, which are specially chosen to complement our world class Biryani selection. All of this is also available together in the form of Combos and Super Saver packs aimed at making the experience of dining with family and friends all the more enjoyable. Amazing Biryani, the best accompanying dishes and good times with family and friends, in short: A Biryani lover's paradise."

Our Story

After graduating from IIT Karakpur,got placed in one of the top auto mobile companies in Gurgoan, that's how i shifted to Gurgoan and got a chance to start Biriyani Feast.Being from south, food is always an inherent part of our daily life,so when i shifted to Gurgoan in 2011 I hardly found options for my south indian cravings.That time people are very much found of butter chicken,pizza, burgers so on.But south indian cravings are much different from the thing of what people eat here. So after a lot of research,I thought in Gurgoan the population of south indians are good enough, and even the people of other parts of indian are well aware of hyderabadi dum biryani.Thats the time when we started our first outlet in sector 23 Gurgoan. I still remember the nervousness of how people will react to the taste of our food but thing went beyond my expectations and we did pretty well from the day one.Customer accepted anonymously,thats the time we understood customers accept any cuisine if they like the taste.Then the same began when we started our second outlet in Gurgoan and the customers supported the same.Day by day Biriyani Feast started becoming bigger and stronger. In eight years we are standing at 50+ outlets across pan India and Dubai.Whichever reason we enter customers started supporting us the same.
